Native Aura

Description: Native Aura is a platform dedicated to celebrating and preserving the rich tribal cultures of Karnataka. By showcasing the history, traditions, crafts, and stories of various tribes, it aims to raise awareness and promote the unique cultural identity of these communities.


Key Features:

  • Detailed profiles of tribal communities with information on their history, crafts, festivals, and traditions.
  • Embedded videos showcasing tribal dance forms, rituals, and cultural performances.
  • E-commerce functionality to purchase specialized tribal products like honey, crafts, jewelry, and textiles.
  • Engaging multimedia content, such as images and videos, for an immersive experience of tribal art and culture.

HelpNow

Description: HelpNow is a virtual first aid room providing immediate guidance and assistance in emergencies. It helps individuals access first aid tips and instructions for various medical situations, empowering users to act confidently during emergencies.


Key Features:

  • Step-by-step first aid instructions for various emergencies, including choking, bleeding, burns, fractures, etc.
  • Video tutorials and images demonstrating first aid techniques for clear understanding.
  • Integration with emergency services for quick access to medical help.
  • User-friendly interface with easy navigation for both experienced and non-experienced users.

Medical Situations Covered:

  • Choking: Instructions on how to perform the Heimlich maneuver and clear an airway obstruction.
  • Bleeding: Steps to control bleeding and perform wound dressing.
  • Burns: First aid for treating burns of different severity levels.
  • Fractures: How to immobilize fractures and prevent further injury.

Interactive Features:

  • Quick Access: Direct links to emergency numbers for fast help.
  • Video Demonstrations: Embedded video tutorials for hands-on guidance.
  • First Aid Kit Checklist: A list of essential items to keep in a first aid kit.
  • Emergency Alerts: Push notifications for important health tips and reminders for specific conditions.

Multimedia Content:

Call to Action:

  • Get First Aid Help: Leads to the interactive first aid guide for various emergencies.
